Cosmic Credit

Industy project with Frog
Creating a finance brand for young people
In collaboration with Ash Growney

Primary research - focus groups Q&A

Affinity diagrams

Website and brand visual moodboard

Logo development

The theming for the brand was based on galaxy and stars. The name needed a reference to financial literacy and the theme. Cosmic Credit embodied the galaxy theme and a recognisable finance term. ‘Credit’ can also refer to giving ownership of content which reflects the brands service of young people giving to other young people.

Brand boards

Website Ideation concept

From primary research insights, young people engage with games, interactive visuals and individual identity. The main idea for the website interface is based on point and click games which encourages exploration with the basic mechanic of clicking on elements.
